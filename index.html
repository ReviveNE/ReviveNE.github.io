Revive Inc.

For a business to succeed long term, you need to have one focus at your core:

There must be a shared benefit between employees, clients, and owners.

Since his first job picking tobacco at 14 years old, through his over 20 year career in corporate America, Brent has been growing his knowledge on what makes a successful business.

In the second half of his life, Brent has decided to take his experience and knowledge and share what he has learned with others looking to build a career and even possibly enter entrepreneurship.

Through his detailing business, Revive Auto Detailing, Brent has built one of the top detail shops in New England that has found success delivering a quality experience for his clients, while developing a staff of the top detailers.

As Brent has raised up new leaders at Revive he has been able to shift his focus to delivering business coaching to entrepreneurs that are the best in their field and benefit from support in business management.
